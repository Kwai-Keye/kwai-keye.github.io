Welcome to Kwai Keye
Our team is dedicated to pushing the boundaries of artificial intelligence, focusing on the cutting-edge exploration and innovative practice of multimodal large language models. We believe that intelligent agents integrating multisource data—such as vision, language, and behavior—are key to unlocking deeper cognition and decision-making. Our research focuses on: short video understanding; multimodal recommendation; reinforcement learning (RL) algorithms; and unified multimodal understanding and generation models.
